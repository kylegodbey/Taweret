Truth

\(f(x) = 2-0.1(x-4)^2\), where \(x \in [-1, 9]\)

Model 1

\(f_1(x,\theta)= 0.5(x+\theta)-2\) , where \(\theta \in [1, 6]\)

Model 2

\(f_2(x,\theta)= -0.5(x-\theta) + 3.7\) , where \(\theta \in [-2, 3]\)

Experimental data

sampled from the Truth with a fixed standard deviation of 0.3
